How to Use the Japan Halal Travel Assistant 🇯🇵🌸

What can I ask?

  • Find Halal Food: "Where can I find Halal ramen in Shinjuku, Tokyo?" or "Are there any Muslim-friendly sushi spots in Kyoto?"
  • Locate Prayers Spaces: "Where is the nearest masjid to Osaka Castle?" or "Is there a prayer room in Narita Airport?"
  • Get Prayer Times: "What time is Maghrib today in Kyoto?" or "What is the sholat schedule for Tokyo?"
  • Itinerary Planning: "Can you build a 3-day Muslim-friendly itinerary for Sapporo?"

Multi-modal Attachments 📎

You can upload images, PDFs, audio, or video alongside your question!

  • Translate Menus: Attach a picture of a restaurant menu and ask: "Is anything on this menu Halal or vegetarian?"
  • Ingredient Checks: Attach a photo of a snack's ingredients and ask: "Does this contain alcohol or pork derivatives?"
